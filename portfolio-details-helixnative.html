Project information

  • Title: Helix Native
  • Category: Software | Plug-Ins
  • Brand: Line 6
  • Project date: January, 2017
  • Project URL: https://line6.com/powercab/
  • Awards: TEC Award Technical Acheivement Nominee (2018)
  • Role: Product Owner

    • Helix Native brings the award-winning guitar multi-effects processing from the Helix hardware products to the computer-based studio. It features preset compatibility to allow for studio to stage (and back again) workflows to allow guitarists to create seamlessly, process audio non-destructively and explore creative options like never before.

  • Contributions: Product Planning, Backlog Management
  • Planned the initial product features, scope and specifications
  • Collaborated with another product manager to ensure preset compatibility
  • Collaborated with another UX designer on UX/UI layout
  • Collaborated with engineering on early prototypes, testing of features, outlining user stories and managing a feature backlog.
  • Collaborated with marketing to create photo content, screen shots
  • Tested prototypes with potential users.
  • Presented an demonstrative overview at AES 2019 trade show in NYC
  • Monitored user feedback for key information about their experience post-launch.
  • Designed a key retail partner point-of-purchase demonstration display elevating sell-thru numbers.
  • Nominated for the prestigious 34th annual TEC Award for Musical Instrument Amplification & Effects (https://www.tecawards.org/nominees/2019/technical)
